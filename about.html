I am a dynamic professional with a unique blend of technical prowess and creative finesse. Holding a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and a diploma in Interactive Media Design, I have harnessed my expertise to excel in the ever-evolving landscape of web app design and development. My passion for crafting innovative digital experiences shines through in my work, where I leverage my adaptability and rapid learning skills to stay at the forefront of technology. With a track record of creating user-friendly interfaces, I have spearheaded projects like "Sous Chef," connecting culinary enthusiasts with chefs, and revitalized the digital presence of establishments such as Sushi Kan and House of Targ. My prowess extends to both design, where I wield tools like Figma and Adobe XD, and development, with proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and more. As a client liaison, I've demonstrated strong relationship management skills, fostering collaboration and ensuring the realization of their product vision. My commitment to excellence is underscored by my inclusion on the Dean's Honors List during my Interactive Media Design diploma at Algonquin College. Whether it's a culinary app, a cocktail page, or a responsive website, I am dedicated to crafting captivating digital solutions that captivate and engage. Furthermore, my professional journey has taken me beyond the realms of design and development. I've honed my skills as a digital marketer, creating and managing campaigns on platforms like Facebook and Instagram. My time at Shabih Film-Making Studio allowed me to delve into the world of video editing and content creation, contributing to the success of the "Mazzeh" cooking show. As an intern at Jitto, I've had the privilege of enhancing the user experience by designing wireframes and improving platform functionality. My diverse skill set, which encompasses coding, graphic design, content management, and multimedia tools, is a testament to my versatility and dedication to delivering high-quality work. In a rapidly evolving digital landscape, I stand out as an adaptable and passionate professional who's always ready to tackle new challenges and create exceptional digital experiences that not only meet but exceed expectations.
